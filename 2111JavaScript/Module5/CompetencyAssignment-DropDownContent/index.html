iPhones I have owned.

iPhone 4s

Obtaining

I first got the iPhone 4s when I was in 8th grade. My mother swapped our phone plan to include data and we were able to get the iPhone 4s as part of that switch. It was really cool to be able to watch youtube on the go with unlimited data.

Forfeiting

The phone lasted until about halfway through 10th grade when the home button fell off. I thought it was interisting the button was just a piece of plastic on top of a regular button.

iPhone 5s

Obtaining

The iPhone 5s was the first phone I had purchased myself. I spent most of the bonus from working at Lagoon on buying the phone. The 5s was super cool because it was the first iPhone to have the fingerprint scanner.

Forfeiting

The death of this phone for me was sad and it was the first smartphone I broke. I accidentally dropped the phone on the tile floor of a bathroom in my house and the screen shattered. I funnily enough kept using the phone until the LCD panel gave out completely.

iPhone 6s Plus

Obtaining

This phone was so freaking large. I got the 6s Plus after the screen on the 5s I had broke. The 6s is still my favorite iPhone to date as I think it was the coolest in design for the time. The only downside of the size and thinness is the bending which occured in almost all of the ones produced.

Forfeiting

This phone lasted all the way until I was able to trade it in towards my next phone. I took amazing care of this phone especially given the fact I had it through most of high school. I remember they gave me full trade in value when I went to get the iPhone X.

iPhone X

Obtaining

This was the first phone Apple made with facial recognition tech. I was stoked to get it because I was going to be able to get the newest phone with this groundbreaking tech. This is the phone that really got me into Apple repairs and hardware specs. This was also the time I was working at Best Buy and was able to do the phone repairs

Forfeiting

Along with the 6s Plus I was able to get max value of the phone for a trade in when I got the 12 pro. I do really miss the design of this phone but I think the boxiness of the 12 and up is much better design for modern design standards.

iPhone 12 Pro

Obtaining

This is my current phone and I absolutely love it. The best part about it is the upgrades Apple made to the wireless charging capabilities with the introduction of the magsafe tech. Its funny to call it tech as its pretty much just a magnet with a small chip reader to tell which accessory is attached to the phone at the time. I honestly think most of the UI effects attached to the adding or removing of MagSafe accessories is extremely gimicky.

Plans for the Future

I really like this phone but I will most likely upgrade this fall when the next generation comes out. I recently have been upgrading every other year and since I skipped the 13 I will probably pull the trigger on the next model. I am excited to see what the next phone has upgrade wise. Still hoping for a variable high-refresh rate OLED screen.
